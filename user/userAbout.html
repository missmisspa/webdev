About Barangay 43-Tamaoyan

Welcome to Barangay 43-Tamaoyan, a vibrant community nestled in the heart of Legazpi City, Albay, Philippines. Formerly known as Barangay 67, our barangay has a rich history and a diverse population, contributing to the dynamic tapestry of Legazpi.

Demographics

With a population of 2,003 as of the 2020 Census, Barangay 43-Tamaoyan is home to a thriving community of individuals and families. Over the years, our population has steadily grown, reflecting a positive growth rate of 4.27% from 2015 to 2020. Our households, numbering 361 in total, boast an average size of 4.55 members, fostering a sense of kinship and camaraderie among residents.

Population Distribution

Our community is characterized by a diverse age distribution, with each age group contributing to the vibrancy of our barangay. From the energetic youth aged 5 to 9, comprising 11.21% of our population, to the wise seniors aged 80 and over, each member of our community adds value and uniqueness to Barangay 43-Tamaoyan.

Location

Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Luzon, Barangay 43-Tamaoyan enjoys a strategic location, with coordinates approximately 13.1731 latitude and 123.7406 longitude. Situated at an elevation of 23.8 meters above mean sea level, our barangay offers breathtaking views and easy access to the natural beauty of Albay.
